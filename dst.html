How DST Scores Work

INcommand of the Message · AI-powered analysis

Overall Score (0–100)

Weighted across 6 INcommand dimensions. For DSR outbound calls, the primary conversation is Uncover Needs — discovery quality and pain identification are weighted heavily. AI Story is weighted at 20% reflecting Intralinks' AI-first positioning.

The 6 Dimensions

  • Opening & Preparation (10%) — Did the rep demonstrate research? Earn the right to ask questions? Establish credibility in the first 30 seconds?
  • Discovery Quality (20%) — Double-sided, open-ended (TED-W) questions. Follow-up and funnel questions. Avoided close-ended, double-barreled, or wordy questions.
  • Pain Identification (20%) — Did the rep uncover a specific pain point across the three value drivers: reducing cost, increasing revenue, mitigating risk?
  • AI Story (20%) — Did the rep lead with AI? SS&C sovereign AI / Link / stack control argument. For Alts: Ask Link in FundCentre + Unified FundCentre Q4 2026 pitch.
  • Objection Handling (10%) — Acknowledge → Message → Challenge framework. Did the rep handle pushback without folding or over-explaining?
  • Next Step (20%) — Specific next step with a date committed. Demo, follow-up call, or proposal. Vague close = 0.

AI Story — What "Sharp" Looks Like

Corporates/B&C: Leads with AI not VDR. SS&C sovereign AI — model, fine-tuning, hosting inside the perimeter. DealCentre Connect / MCP. Datasite differentiation (Blueflame outsourced vs. built in-house).

Alts: Ask Link embedded in FundCentre. Unified FundCentre Q4 2026 — one platform for fundraising, onboarding, reporting. Cross-module analytics — LP relationship lifecycle view. Anchor pitch: "Your LPs are getting more demanding, your competitors are professionalizing their investor experience..."

Meeting Outcome

Primary success metric for DSR calls. Scored as: Meeting Booked / Follow-up Committed / No Next Step.
